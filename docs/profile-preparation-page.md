## Subtitle
Before you start, here's what you'll need to complete your profile and get listed in our creative directory.

---

## Section 1: Images

### Profile Photo
Your profile photo appears throughout the directory and helps other members recognise you.

**Requirements:**
- Square format works best (we'll crop if needed)
- Minimum 400 x 400 pixels
- Clear, well-lit image
- Professional headshot OR your logo for businesses

**Tips:**
- Use a recent photo that represents you well
- Avoid busy backgrounds
- Show your face clearly (for individuals)

### Header/Feature Image
This larger image appears at the top of your profile page and should showcase your work or creative space.

**Requirements:**
- Landscape format (16:9 recommended)
- Minimum 1200 x 675 pixels
- High quality, visually engaging

**Tips:**
- Show your best work, studio, or creative space
- Choose an image that tells your story
- Avoid text-heavy images

---

## Section 2: About You

### Bio (Required)
Tell the community about yourself, your practice, and what you do.

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 50 characters
- Recommended 100-300 words

**Include:**
- What you do / your creative practice
- Your background and experience
- What makes you unique
- Who you work with or what you create

**Example:**
> "I'm a ceramicist based in Katoomba, creating functional pottery inspired by the Australian bush. After 15 years in graphic design, I transitioned to clay work in 2018, finding joy in the tactile nature of the medium. My pieces are wheel-thrown and feature native plant motifs using locally-sourced materials where possible."

### Business/Trading Name (For businesses)
If you operate under a business name, you'll enter this during setup.
